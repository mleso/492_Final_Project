User Guide

This map is designed to help you explore Oregon's historical wine production.

American Viticulture Areas, or AVAs, are designated wine-growing regions in the United States that have unique geographical and climatic characteristics. Each AVA has terrain and influences site selection, fruit quality, and farming practices.

Click the Red Button in the upper right corner to begin, and click the wine glass markers to learn more about each winery. Use "Click to Explore" to fly between locations, or "Reset Map" to return to the start.

AVA boundary data courtesy of the U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB).
